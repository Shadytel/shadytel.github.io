About Us

We are the 501(c)(3) non-profit behind Shadytel.

Part independent research lab, part museum, NT&T supports the scientific and educational goals of Shadytel. We also provide charitable contributions to independent information security researchers in need. Program activities include:

  • Acquiring and maintaining historic telecom equipment for public use
  • Educational activities around historic telecom equipment
  • Educational activities around electronics and computer networks
  • Scientific research and development in amateur radio, electronics, telephony systems, and computer networks
  • Space and equipment for our members' scientific research and development needs
  • Services for the amateur radio community
